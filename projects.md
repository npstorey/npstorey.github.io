## NYC Agency Dataset

Built the City's first standardized open dataset of NYC agencies, consolidating 8 fragmented agency lists into a public reference of 303 entities. This dataset now powers the NYC.gov Agency Directory.

## MetaCat

Co-developed an internal metadata catalog for NYC government containing 325+ data assets across 21 agencies, improving data discovery and governance across city operations.

## NYC Open Data AI Tools

Created open-source tools that enable AI systems to query NYC Open Data APIs, making city data more accessible through natural language interfaces.

## Street Homeless Solutions Lab

Founded a user-centered design lab at the NYC Department of Homeless Services, focused on improving services for street homeless New Yorkers through participatory research and policy co-creation.

## WorkerConnect Training

Led training for 400+ city staff on WorkerConnect, a cross-agency data-sharing platform that improves coordination across NYC government agencies.
